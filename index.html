What can we apply FINPLAN for?

Assess financial viability of power projects by analyzing costs, revenues, and funding sources over time.

Compare financing strategies, such as loans, equity, or grants, and their impacts on project performance.

Evaluate cash flow, debt service, and profitability using financial statements and ratios.

Identify risks related to financing, including sensitivity to interest rates, tariffs, or cost overruns.

Support investment decisions by showing whether a project is bankable or financially sustainable.
